Our Services

Welcome to our Physiotherapy Center

We provide a wide range of physiotherapy services to help you recover and improve your physical health.

Back pain is often caused by poor posture, injuries, or spinal issues. Physiotherapy helps relieve tension and improve mobility.

Neck pain can result from muscle strain or nerve compression. Therapy focuses on stretching, strengthening, and posture correction.

Knee pain may stem from arthritis, injuries, or overuse. Treatment includes manual therapy, exercises, and mobility work.

Pelvic floor therapy addresses bladder control, pain, and core stability, often after childbirth or surgery.

Rehabilitation aids recovery from injury or surgery by restoring strength, movement, and independence through guided therapy.

Physiotherapy for arthritis reduces joint stiffness and pain, improving function through tailored exercise and joint care.

This wrist condition causes pain near the thumb. Therapy includes splinting, stretching, and reducing inflammation.

PLID affects the lower spine, causing leg pain and numbness. Treatment includes traction, core strengthening, and posture correction.
